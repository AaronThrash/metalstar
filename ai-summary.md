## What is Metalstar?

Metalstar is an original science fiction mythology that began in 1989 as a comic book. Set in the 24th century, it follows three heroes — Sarah, John, and Jayk — who are chosen to defend Earth against alien invaders called the Sky Reapers. These deadly creatures seek to reengineer Earth's environment using gardens that grow alien vegetation. Guardian aliens known as the Nephelum bestow upon the heroes the Metal Stars, extraordinary devices that utilize zero-point energy — the energy found between atoms — the greatest source of power in the universe.

## Content

The story is published as a serialized novel with 17 chapters currently available to read for free:

1. **Dark Epoch** — Jayk navigates a barren wasteland while Sarah, a clone, is born into a world she was never meant to see.
2. **Sour Ichor** — John D'Arby awakens in a cloning facility with no memory, guided by a robotic engineer named ROGER.
3. **Wachichu** — John learns harsh truths about his existence as a clone and the world that created him.
4. **Sky Reapers** — The alien threat is revealed as deadly creatures descend upon the remnants of civilization.
5. **Baroque Fantasia** — Strange alien flora transforms the landscape into something beautiful and terrifying.
6. **Secret Weapon** — Sarah discovers her extraordinary abilities and the weapon fused to her body.
7. **Sid Mantelis** — A mysterious figure emerges with knowledge of the old world and dangerous ambitions.
8. **New Beginnings** — Po and the survivors forge new alliances in a changed world.
9. **Charged Particles** — Rensa and the science of zero-point energy take center stage.
10. **Solos** — A journey through an abandoned city reveals the scale of civilization's collapse.
11. **Wasteland** — The brutal reality of survival in the endless desert.
12. **The Free City** — Solos, a haven of trade and freedom, becomes a turning point.
13. **The Prophecy** — Trinidad reveals an ancient prophecy that binds the heroes to their destiny.
14. **Spice Road** — Desert traders and the dangerous routes between settlements.
15. **Trade Master** — Anjari and the economics of survival in a post-apocalyptic world.
16. **The Mountain** — A treacherous journey to a place of power and revelation.
17. **Prizefight** — Ivan and a brutal contest that will determine the fate of many.
